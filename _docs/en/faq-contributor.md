## Do I need to be assigned before I start?
Usually yes. If you start work before approval, you risk duplicating effort or working against a different expected scope.

## What counts as “done”?
Typically: your Pull Request matches the task requirements, passes automated checks (if any), and is accepted by the maintainer.

## When do I get paid?
Payment is released after acceptance according to the project’s workflow. See [payments](/docs/en/payments).

## What if I get stuck?
Share what you tried, what you observed, and ask specific questions on the issue/PR.
