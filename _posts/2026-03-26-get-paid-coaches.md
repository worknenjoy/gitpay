## The problem: Coaches chasing payments

Even after the work is delivered, payment can stall because the next step is unclear (invoice, approvals, finance queue, international transfer, etc.). The result is the same: you spend time following up instead of doing more billable work.

## Why traditional invoicing slows you down

- Invoices look like paperwork, not an action
- Clients need internal approvals (and your invoice gets stuck)
- International clients add extra steps and fees
- Small projects shouldn’t require heavy accounting setup

## A simpler workflow: payment requests

1. Finish the work
2. Create a payment request
3. Send the link
4. Client pays

## Example workflow (copy/paste)

> Delivered the agreed milestone and shared the handoff notes. You can pay using this payment link: (Gitpay payment request link). Thanks!

## Example use cases for Coaches

- Single session payment
- 4-week coaching package
- Accountability program
- Group coaching seat
